Basic Operations

Table 1 lists the basic operations supported by the WebUI of the edge management system.

Table 1 Basic operations

Operation

Description

Viewing system information

Choose Home > More.

The System Information page displays the basic information and peripherals of the device. The basic information includes the hostname, version, OS, product model, and product SN.

Viewing online help

On the WebUI of the edge management system, click on the upper right corner.

Changing a password

After logging in to the WebUI of the edge management system, move the mouse pointer to on the upper right corner of the page.

Click Change Password to change the password of the current user.

NOTE:

In this version, when the password of a web account is changed, the backend password of the web account is not changed accordingly.

Logging out

Move the mouse pointer to on the upper right corner of the page.

Click Log Out to exit the WebUI.
